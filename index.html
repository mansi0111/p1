About Us

Public speaking is all about enabling your tongue to deliver the message of your heart. We are a group of inquisitive individuals with an unremunerative goal of catalyzing the growth of peoples’ life and career by projecting a unique learning journey towards building Charismatic, inspiring individuals with an ability to create instant rapport with their audience in the field of Public Speaking. We are believers of,

"If you can Speak,you can Influence,and if you can Influence, you can change lives."

We plan to conduct weekly sessions, mentoring students with individual attention, and helping you develop your on-stage persona with the help of multi-faceted activities like group discussions, opinionated talks, debates, oratorical sessions, etc..

Come Join us in this exciting journey. We are Open to applications from both mentors as well as learners.
